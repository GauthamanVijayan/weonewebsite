The Foundation of Trust

Building a verified network, one household at a time.

Field Officer Verification Process

WEONE is built on a structured verification process conducted by field officers. These officers visit households ward-by-ward to verify residents against official voter lists, register family members, and assist with app installation.

Verified residents receive a digital badge, confirming their authenticity. This process eliminates misinformation and ensures that all interactions on the platform are with real, accountable members of the community.
